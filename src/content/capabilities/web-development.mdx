      <h2>How We Build Websites That Hold Up</h2>

      <h3>Clean, Intentional Code Structure</h3>
      <p>
        Every website we build is structured to be readable, organized, and intentional — not rushed or overcomplicated.
      </p>
      <p>This means:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Consistent patterns instead of one-off fixes</li>
        <li>Reusable components instead of duplicated code</li>
        <li>Clear separation between content, layout, and functionality</li>
      </ul>
      <p>
        A clean structure makes the site easier to maintain, safer to update, and far less likely to break as it evolves.
      </p>

      <h3>Performance Built Into the Foundation</h3>
      <p>
        Speed isn't something we "optimize later." It's influenced by decisions made during development.
      </p>
      <p>We pay attention to:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>How assets are loaded</li>
        <li>How scripts are used</li>
        <li>How much work the browser is asked to do</li>
        <li>How pages respond across devices and connections</li>
      </ul>
      <p>
        Rather than chasing perfect benchmarks, we focus on real-world performance that stays consistent as the site grows.
      </p>

      <h3>Built to Change Without Breaking</h3>
      <p>
        Most websites don't fail immediately — they fail when changes are made.
      </p>
      <p>We develop websites so:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>New pages can be added without disrupting existing ones</li>
        <li>Updates don't introduce fragile dependencies</li>
        <li>Features can be expanded without rewriting the system</li>
      </ul>
      <p>
        This allows your website to evolve naturally instead of requiring rebuilds every time something changes.
      </p>

      <h3>Security Considered From the Start</h3>
      <p>
        Security isn't just about protecting data — it's about protecting reliability.
      </p>
      <p>Our development approach follows established best practices to:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Reduce common vulnerabilities</li>
        <li>Limit unnecessary exposure</li>
        <li>Keep the system predictable and controlled</li>
      </ul>
      <p>
        This reduces risk over time and helps prevent small issues from becoming major problems.
      </p>

      <h3>Compatibility With Modern Tools and Services</h3>
      <p>
        Many websites rely on outside tools — forms, analytics, scheduling, payments, or third-party platforms.
      </p>
      <p>We build with integration in mind so your website can:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Connect with tools you already use</li>
        <li>Adopt new tools later without friction</li>
        <li>Avoid brittle hacks or shortcuts</li>
      </ul>

      </p>
